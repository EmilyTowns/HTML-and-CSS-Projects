The Autumn Wind

Authentic Sail Boat Tours in Historic Lunenburg

About:

The Autumn Wind is a scooner built in 1959 by Lunenburg Ship Builders Inc.

I has been in races in four continents and was retired in 1993.

In 1995, The Autumn Wind was refurbished and retrofited with Briggs and Stratton Engines and began her life as a touring vessel.

We have regular tours of the Nova Scotia coast along with special events and can be hired for private events as well.
